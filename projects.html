PORTFOLIO

Recently I've decided to embrace digital minimalism. Instead of carrying my phone around with me everywhere, now I only take my Apple Watch and airpods.

As a Chess fan, I still wanted a way to review my games while I was on the go, but an app for this purpose did not exist for the Apple Watch.

This is what kick-started my coding journey. Christmas 2023 I went all out and built this very app. It now has over 300 downloads, you can check it out here!
