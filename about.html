ABOUT ME

Born in Newfoundland, raised in Nova Scotia, I moved to Toronto in 2015 to pursue a career in Film & Television.

I've worked as an editor, as a visual effects artist, and as a producer. I've worked on some cool music videos, some great short films, and even an award winning feature!

I love the mechanical quality of filmmaking. The engineering of the camera equipment, the way in which each scene of a film takes you from one place to the next -- like a conveyer belt -- processing you along the way.

Software development satisfies this same 'mechanical' urge for me and my lego brain.
Writing a computer program is just building a machine out of code.



Steve Cutler
2024
